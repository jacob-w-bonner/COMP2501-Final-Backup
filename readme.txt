DESCRIPTION:
You are an OC Transpo bus in space that is trying to reach pluto on its maiden voyage. SpaceX is
trying to thwart your journey and are sending all manner of starlink satellites to accomplish this goal.
The goal is to reach mars before SpaceX can kill you, or before you can run out of fuel. Killing enemies
can replenish your fuel or health, or even provide power ups.

CONTROLS:
W -> Move forward
A -> Move left
S -> Move to backwards
D -> Move right
spacebar -> fires tire forward to deal damage to enemies, faster and more projectiles when powered up
lshift -> launches fuel can forward that detonates, dealing damage to enemies in area (not available when powered up)

CRITERIA:
Game Mechanics: final "destroy the boss" challenge at end of game
Enemies: 6 different enemies, all with different movement/behaviour/weapons
Weapons: player can throw a tire or detonate a gas cannister, powerup makes shooting attack more powerful
Movement: transformations utilized on all sprites, enemies have variation of physical and parametric motion
Collision Detection: detection on bullet hit, player hits enemy, player picks up item, etc.
UI: health bar and amount of time until fuel runs out shown, powerup timer shown when powered up
Advanced method: collision resolution when player strikes asteroid
